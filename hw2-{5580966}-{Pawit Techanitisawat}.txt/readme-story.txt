The game story has similarity to the Mario game. 

The main characters in this game are Mario, the princess, monsters.

Objects in this game: floors, rooms, swords, sheilds, portions (mushroom: increase the size, HP and damage strength of Mario), key 




The purpose of this game is that Mario has to find and fight monsters to get to the princess. The princess 

will be in the final stage of the game along with the boss monster, Mario has to fight the final boss first

in order to get the princess. After successfully killed the boss, in order for Mario to get the princess, 

Mario must have the key to unlock the door which the princess is in in order to get her out. This key 

Mario has to find it along the way.
